## Subject

Written data-use permission request — DeepStateMap control-geometry for a non-commercial, static Atlas surface

## Body

Hello DeepStateMap team,

I am building **khazana**, a personal, $0-cost, static/serverless "Atlas" surface. It is
**private today and public-ready** — it may become publicly viewable in the future, and I want
any use of your data to be explicitly authorized in writing before that happens.

I would like to request **explicit written terms** for potentially incorporating DeepStateMap
front-line / control geometry into khazana's automated mapping pipeline. I am **not** asserting
any current right to your data: today khazana only **links out** to DeepStateMap and uses
independent fallback geometry. This message is a request, not a claim of permission.

Specifically, I am asking you to confirm, in writing, whether you can authorize — and on what
terms — each of the following:

1. **Automated retrieval** — periodic programmatic fetch of control geometry.
2. **Private retention / storage** — storing retrieved geometry in a private repository.
3. **Transformation / rendering / embedding** — re-styling and rendering control geometry as a
   map layer inside khazana (client-visible), or, if that is not permitted, a **static rendered
   derivative** instead.
4. **Attribution requirements** — the exact credit line, logo, and link-back you require.
5. **Update cadence / refresh limits** — how frequently automated retrieval is allowed.
6. **Restrictions on client-visible map data** — any limits on what may be exposed to viewers.
7. **Retention & revocation** — how long we may retain data and how you can revoke access.
8. **Private vs. future-public use** — whether any future **public** rendering would require a
   **separate** approval.

To be explicit about what khazana will **not** do: it will **not** proxy or re-expose your API,
will **not** operate a raw-data download browser, will **not** resell your data, and will **not**
represent your assessments as its own. If you cannot authorize embedded / client-visible geometry,
I would like to know whether a **static rendered derivative** is permitted; otherwise I will keep
khazana's current **link-out-only** posture.

Please let me know the correct licensing route if this form is not it, and any terms or fees that
apply. Thank you for your work and for considering this request.

— khazana (founder)
